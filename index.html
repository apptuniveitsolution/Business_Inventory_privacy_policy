Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2025  ·  Apptunive IT Solution

This Privacy Policy explains how Apptunive IT Solution ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Business Inventory mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Business Inventory is designed to operate primarily on your device. We collect minimal data:

  • Locally Stored Data: All inventory items, bills, and business data you enter are stored locally on your device using an on-device database. This data does not leave your device.
  • Contact Information: When you use our contact form or reach us via email, we receive your name and message.
  • Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics to improve app performance (e.g., crash reports).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Business Inventory app's core functionality
  • To respond to support requests sent to our email
  • To improve the app based on anonymous usage patterns
  • To send important app updates (if you opt in)

3. Data Storage & Security

Your business data (products, invoices, customers) is stored entirely on your local device. We do not upload your business data to any cloud server. You are responsible for backing up your device data.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect any data we do process, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

Business Inventory integrates with the following third-party services:

  • WhatsApp: When you choose to share a bill via WhatsApp, you are redirected to the WhatsApp application. Please review WhatsApp's own Privacy Policy for how they handle shared content.
  • Google Play Store: If you download the app via Google Play, Google's privacy policy applies to that transaction.

5. GST & Financial Data

All GST calculations, invoice amounts, and financial records are processed and stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your financial or business data.

6. Children's Privacy

Business Inventory is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

  • Since your data is stored locally, you can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.
  • You may contact us to request deletion of any information we may hold about you.
  • You may opt out of any future communications at any time.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
